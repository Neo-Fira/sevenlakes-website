What to pack for a Haft Kul tour

The packing list for Seven Lakes is short, but the right basics make a day trip much more comfortable on the road and at the higher lakes.

published 2026-03-10 Seven Lakes Tajikistan practical guide
Open tour Seven Lakes guide

What to know before you go

Below you will find the key details that help plan the trip and understand the route more clearly.

Core items

Travelers mainly need documents, practical shoes, water and a weather layer.

The route is not gear-heavy, but comfort improves when the basics are prepared correctly.

Key points

A short summary for travelers who want the main points at a glance.

  • Documents, comfortable shoes, water and an extra layer matter most.
  • The route usually does not require heavy hiking gear.
  • A power bank and enough phone storage are useful for a long day of photos.
