The route step by step

The usual route begins in Samarkand, continues to the Panjakent border area and then moves into the Shing Valley.

The exact timing depends on season, road conditions and the number of planned stops.

Why a private car matters

A private car keeps the day route efficient and gives enough flexibility to reach the stronger photo and viewpoint stops.

That matters more than people expect when trying to see all seven lakes in one day.
